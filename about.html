About us

Fenfen's Barbershop has been in business for over 20 years and offers world-class haircuts and shaves.
We are known for our mix culture barber shop vibe offering classic shaves and modern haircut styling.
Our staff has gone through extensive training. Mastering virtually every hairstyle. Whether you want a vintage, classic,
or modern look, we're the barber shop for you!

Every haircut and shave is executed with precision and style. We promise you'll walk out of here lookin' fresh. We apply a hot towel and pre-shave cream to ensure you get the closest and most comfortable shave possible.

Beards and moustaches are in. We will make sure your burly friend is tamed and styled to look better than ever..
